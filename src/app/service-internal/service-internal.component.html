Timber Systems

Make the best of your budget. At Gordon Mitchell Contractors we work with our clients – and architects – to design and build bespoke timber frames for supply across the UK.

We offer a range of services from fabrication to supply to the full-scale build. Distance is no object – we can deliver across the length and breadth of the UK, including the islands.

We carry out the whole process – from the design to the fabrication of the timber frame kits – on site, so you can literally see your build evolve from start to finish.

 

Attention to detail and quality workmanship are the hallmarks of our timber frames. You can relax in the knowledge that our bespoke timber frame kits are exceptionally well-made, slotting and fitting together precisely.
