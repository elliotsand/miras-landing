Nosotros Principios Equipo

Somos una empresa peruana de ingeniería, compuesta por profesionales con amplia experiencia en el diseño, dirección, supervisión y desarrollo de proyectos de Ingeniería.

Nuestro equipo ha participado en el desarrollo de proyectos de gran envergadura y ha gestionado de manera eficiente proyectos en los cuales ha sido partícipe.

Nuestra empresa cuenta con una experiencia vasta en los diferentes rubros de infraestructura teniendo un valor agregado de poder realizar la consultoría y poder ejecutar su propio producto con la ventaja de reducir costos, tiempos, y minimizar las restricciones dentro de la ejecución de los proyectos.

nuestros principios

We undertake a host of diverse projects, spanning a range of scopes and scales. Each and every one of these is treated with the same core values that characterise our company – honesty, integrity, professional advice and quality craftsmanship. In fact, honesty and trust are the very cornerstones of our business.

We always strive to build homes that match our clients’ expectations, but we don’t shy away from offering realistic advice when it’s needed.

We are just as honest about our costings – we guarantee that any quote we provide will be completely comprehensive. There are no hidden costs here, which means you have an accurate understanding of how your budget will work for you.

Our people

People are at the heart of Gordon Mitchell Contractors – both the people who have entrusted us with building their homes, and our highly dedicated and skilled team who are turning their vision into reality.

Each and every member of our staff shares our ethos and takes pride in their work. We invest a great deal in developing and nurturing our team’s knowledge and skills, helping them to retain and pass on traditional skills and methodologies. We know that, at the end of the day, our buildings are only as good as the team that builds them.

But we are equally passionate about marrying these approaches to the contemporary designs and techniques of the modern-day industry.
